Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Program hours
    • Illinois State requires 350 hours of training and it will take approximately 3-6 months to finish and it all depends on your input with our classes.

  2. When will the new class start?
    • The school has an open enrollment, so you can start anytime after the $600 down payment is paid.

  3. How to enroll in school?
    • We recommend that the student should stop by and see the school operation and we are ready to answer all the questions needed before any decisions made to enroll with us.

  4. How does the payment work?
    • You can either pay the full tuition fee , or we have a payment plans for your options. The down payment for the plans is $600 and you will make a monthly plans to pay up the rest without any interests.

  5. What are the class schedules?
    • We open weekly Monday-Friday 10am-8pm, Saturday 10am-7pm. You can come anytime and as frequent as possible to finish up the 350 hours training courses. We have a very flexible class schedules according to your time and availability.

If you have more questions about our school we are here to answer. Reach out to us and we'll respond as soon as we can.
